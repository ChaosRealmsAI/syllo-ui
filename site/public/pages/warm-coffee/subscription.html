FAQ · Common Questions

Can I pause my membership anytime?

Yes. You can pause for up to 30 days by setting it at least three days before any billing cycle. One pause per cycle.

What delivery areas are supported?

All domestic cities support store pickup. Some cities also support courier delivery—see the order page for details.

How do I cancel auto-renewal?

Go to Membership Settings in your profile and toggle off auto-renewal. Existing benefits remain available until your current cycle ends.
